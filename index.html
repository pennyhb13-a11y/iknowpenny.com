Loan Programs

A loan for your situation. Whatever that looks like.

First-time buyer, self-employed, veteran, no down payment — there's a program. Here's the actual rate-sheet view, not a marketing summary.

Zero & Low Down
ProgramDownWhat it's forNotes
VA Loan 0% down For active military and veterans. No PMI, competitive rates — one of the best deals in lending if you've earned it. Conforming¹
USDA Loan 0% down Zero down for eligible rural and suburban properties. Most people never hear about this one. Area-based
FHA Loan 3.5% down Flexible credit requirements, backed by the federal government. Good for first-time buyers building their footing. $1,029,250²
DPA Programs Varies Tennessee offers several down payment assistance programs. Most buyers qualify for more than they expect. Program-based

ProgramDownWhat it's forLimit
20% Down 20% down Instant equity, no PMI, lowest monthly payment. Ideal if you have the savings and want the best long-term position. $832,750
